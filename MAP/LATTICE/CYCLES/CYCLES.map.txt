===========================
  LATTICE CYCLES
===========================

CORE EXECUTION CYCLE:
1) aTom    define atomic logic
2) SPACE   assemble modules
3) TIME    schedule/sequence
4) ENERGY  execute runtime
5) MOTION  express behaviours

DATA/ASSET CYCLE:
SPACE   MATTER  SPACE
Modules read/write assets, remain bound to identity + domain.

IDENTITY CYCLE:
WALLET   RESOLVER  DOMAIN  SPACE  TIME
Identity anchors all execution and state.

CONFIG CYCLE:
VOID  (TIME, SPACE, aTom, ENERGY, MATTER, MOTION, IDENTITY)
Config and secrets modulate every layer without carrying logic themselves.

STATUS:
All cycles are now explicitly mapped and bound into the lattice.
